## Overview

A1 Education is dedicated to offering top-notch educational support through personalized tutoring sessions, tailored programs, and a wealth of resources. The mission is to empower students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed academically and beyond.

## Features

- **Personalized Tutoring**: One-on-one tutoring sessions tailored to individual student needs.
- **Comprehensive Programs**: A variety of programs covering different subjects and levels.
- **Experienced Tutors**: A team of highly qualified and experienced tutors.
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Convenient scheduling options to fit busy lifestyles.
- **Resource Center**: Access to a range of educational materials and resources.

## Programs Offered

- **Primary Education**: Foundational programs for younger students to build essential skills.
- **Secondary Education**: Advanced tutoring in various subjects for high school students.
- **Exam Preparation**: Specialized programs to help students prepare for important exams.
- **Homework Help**: Assistance with daily homework and assignments.

## Getting Started

To get started with A1 Education, follow these steps:

1. **Visit the Website**: Go to [A1 Education](https://a1education.com.au/).
2. **Explore Programs**: Browse through the different programs and services offered.
3. **Contact Us**: Use the contact form or call to get in touch and discuss specific needs.
4. **Enroll**: Enroll in the desired program and schedule your first session.

## Usage

1. **Navigating the Website**:
   - Use the main navigation menu to explore different sections of the website.
   - Click on "Programs" to see the detailed offerings and descriptions.
   - Visit the "Contact Us" page for inquiries and further information.

2. **Booking a Session**:
   - Choose the program that best suits your needs.
   - Fill out the enrollment form or contact the office directly to schedule a session.

3. **Accessing Resources**:
   - Log in to the student portal to access educational resources and materials.
   - Utilize the resource center for additional support and learning materials.

## Project Structure

The project structure is as follows:

```
a1education/
│
├── css/
│   ├── styles.css
│
├── js/
│   └── main.js
│
├── index.html
│
├── about.html
│
├── contact.html
│
├── programs.html
│
└── assets/
    └── images/
        └── logo.png
        └── banner.jpg
```

- `index.html`: The main homepage of the website.
- `about.html`: Information about A1 Education and the mission.
- `contact.html`: Contact information and form.
- `programs.html`: Detailed descriptions of the programs offered.
- `css/styles.css`: Custom CSS for styling the website.
- `js/main.js`: JavaScript file containing functionality for the website.
- `assets/images/`: Directory containing images used on the website.

## Technologies Used

- **HTML**: The structure of the website.
- **CSS**: Styling of the website using custom CSS.
- **JavaScript**: Functionality and interactivity of the website.
